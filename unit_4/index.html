The Swiss Army Knife Of Marketing

Who is Thomas

Currently based in Orange County California but raised in the green fields of the UK. Thomas is a marketing all rounder that can assist in all aspects of marketing for your business. From Social media to Web Site design and everything in between.

Why Thomas

Thomas ran a marketing department in a large national company for over 3 years where he refined this skills and became a man of all trades and excelled at everything that was thrown at him. He has a “job done” attitude towards work but believes every project must have a fun element to it.

More about Thomas

Thomas lives with his wife and growing family in Huntington Beach, when he is not working he enjoys long walks with his wife and dog on the beach. Thomas always thought Americans were strange for drinking Iced Tea as there’s nothing worst then drinking a cup of tea that has gone cold, he understands now!

Testimonials

Hired Thomas for a number of projects including marketing materials to help my small business. Thomas is responsive, hardworking and always understands the needs of my business.

Worked along Thomas in the previous industry we both worked in, One of the most professional members of that industry.
